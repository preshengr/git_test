Coquito

Description

Coquito is a festive beverage from Puerto Rico that is creamy, tropical, and bursting with coconut flavour. Condensed and evaporated milk, cream of coconut, spices, rum, and, in this variation, egg yolks are the ingredients. At my holiday parties, people always ask for it.

I occasionally keep it for myself by hiding it in the refrigerator! If desired, top with additional cinnamon and serve in glass cups. Happy Navidad!

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 large egg yolks, beaten
  • 1 (14 ounce) can cream of coconut
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • ½ cup white rum
  • ½ cup water
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
